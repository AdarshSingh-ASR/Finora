# Category policy

- Food & Dining: restaurants, groceries, cafés, and food delivery.
- Housing: rent, maintenance, and home services.
- Transport: local transit, rides, fuel, tolls, and routine rail travel.
- Shopping: retail goods and e-commerce where a more specific category is unavailable.
- Bills & Utilities: power, water, gas, phone, broadband, and routine household bills.
- Health: medical care, pharmacy, fitness, and health subscriptions.
- Entertainment: streaming, cinema, games, and leisure media.
- Travel: flights, hotels, and non-routine trip expenses.
- EMI: recurring loan repayments; keep separate from ordinary bills.
- Investment: SIPs, funds, broker deposits, and other wealth contributions; exclude from consumption.
- Salary: payroll inflows explicitly identified as salary.
- Income: interest, refunds, and other inflows that are not salary.
- Transfers: account-to-account movement and person-to-person transfers whose purpose is not known.
- Miscellaneous: ordinary consumption with insufficient evidence for a more specific category.
- Other: reserve for raw or not-yet-categorized transactions.

Treat user-confirmed corrections as confidence 1.0. Never learn a global rule from one correction unless the user asks.
